What's the recommended sag for my rear shock?
For the RockShox Vivid 2024 on your Stumpjumper EVO, the recommended sag is:
Riding StyleSag %Method
Trail / All-Mountain25-30%Seated, riding gear
Enduro / Aggressive30-35%Standing attack position
With the 210x55mm stroke, that puts you at roughly 14-16.5mm of sag measurement at the shock body.

Set it with the blue low-speed compression fully open (counter-clockwise) to start, then add 2-3 clicks once sag is set.
[RockShox Vivid Service Manual, Pages 12-14]

How do I bleed my brakes?
Here's the bleed procedure for your Hayes Dominion A4:

Tools needed: Hayes bleed kit, DOT 4 fluid, T10 Torx, 8mm wrench

  1. Remove wheel and pads. Insert bleed block.
  2. Attach syringe to caliper bleed port (T10 Torx).
  3. Remove lever reservoir cap, attach upper syringe.
  4. Push fluid from caliper up through lever — slow, steady pressure.
  5. Tap lever body and hose to dislodge air bubbles.
  6. Close caliper port first, then lever port.
  7. Pump lever 10x to check firmness. Repeat if spongy.
[Hayes Dominion Service Manual, Pages 18-22]
